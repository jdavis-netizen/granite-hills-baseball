Brenden Lewis Named Union-Tribune Boys Athlete of the Year

Senior Brenden Lewis was named the San Diego Union-Tribune's 2024-25 Boys Athlete of the Year. The two-time SD Section Player of the Year hit .400 with 16 RBI and 5 home runs in his final season, finishing his career with a .350 average, 122 hits, and 107 runs in 108 games. Lewis, who carried a 4.2 GPA, is headed to Arizona State. Read more →

June 3, 2025

Eagles' Undefeated Run Ends in SoCal Regional

Granite Hills' historic unbeaten season came to an end with a heartbreaking 5-4 loss in nine innings to Villa Park in the CIF SoCal Regional playoffs. Tommy Entrekin hit a two-run homer and Brenden Lewis had two singles and scored twice, but Villa Park rallied in the 7th and 9th innings to end the Eagles' remarkable 24-game unbeaten streak. The Eagles finished 24-1-2. Read more →

June 1, 2025

Undefeated Eagles Earn No. 4 Seed in SoCal Regionals

Despite going 24-0-2 and winning the CIF San Diego Section Open Division title, the Eagles received the No. 4 seed in the CIF SoCal Regional baseball playoffs. Granite Hills became the first team in San Diego Section history to go unbeaten through the regular season and playoffs. Read more →

Eagles Win CIF San Diego Section Open Division Championship!

Parker Vance's bases-loaded triple in the sixth inning broke a scoreless tie as Granite Hills beat Patrick Henry 3-0 at Tony Gwynn Stadium (SDSU) to claim the Open Division title. Luke Bailey threw a dominant complete game shutout, striking out 9 with his fastball touching 93 mph. The win improved the Eagles to 24-0-2. Read more →

Eagles Eliminate St. Augustine to Reach CIF Finals

The top-ranked Eagles raced to a 6-0 lead and held on to beat St. Augustine 6-4 in the Upper Bracket final at Granite Hills Field. Parker Vance launched a tape-measure home run over the center field fence, and Brenden Lewis contributed an RBI single while pitching five solid innings. Trevor Smith added a 2-run homer. May 16, 2025

Eagles Make History with Undefeated Regular Season

Granite Hills finished the regular season 20-0-2, becoming the first Grossmont Conference team since 1962 to post an unbeaten record and the first Grossmont Hills League team to win the championship without a loss. The Eagles held a 125-10 scoring advantage in their 12 league wins, finishing well ahead of runner-up Helix (7-5). Read more →

2025

Five Eagles Headed to Play College Ball

Five members of the 2025 championship team signed to continue their athletic careers at the collegiate level: Brenden Lewis (Arizona State), Trevor Smith (Washington State), Tommy Entrekin (Cal Baptist), Luke Bailey (UC Santa Barbara), and Parker Vance (Southern Utah, football). The Eagles program continues its tradition of developing student-athletes for the next level.
