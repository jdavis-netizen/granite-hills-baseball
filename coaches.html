Coaching Staff

The leaders behind Granite Hills Eagles Baseball

⚾

Coach James Williams

Head Coach

Coach Williams is in his 12th year leading the Eagles program. A former collegiate player, he has guided Granite Hills to three league championships and multiple playoff appearances. He emphasizes fundamentals, discipline, and character development.

🏀

Coach Mike Torres

Assistant Coach / Pitching

Coach Torres oversees the pitching staff and bullpen development. A former minor league pitcher, he brings professional-level knowledge of mechanics and pitch strategy to the Eagles rotation.

🥊

Coach David Chen

Assistant Coach / Hitting

Coach Chen runs the Eagles' hitting program, focusing on approach, swing mechanics, and situational hitting. His data-driven methods have helped improve the team's offensive production year over year.

🏃

Coach Robert Jackson

Assistant Coach / Defense & Base Running

Coach Jackson handles infield and outfield defensive work as well as base running strategy. He is known for his high-energy coaching style and attention to detail on the fundamentals.

📋

Coach Sarah Miller

JV Head Coach

Coach Miller leads the JV program, developing underclassmen for varsity competition. She focuses on player development and creating a positive, competitive environment for young Eagles.

💪

Coach Alex Ruiz

Strength & Conditioning

Coach Ruiz designs and runs the team's strength and conditioning program. His training protocols focus on injury prevention, athletic performance, and building the physical foundation for success on the field.
